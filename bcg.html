Blue cheese gnocchi

Creamy and soothing, this warming bake makes a great standby for friends and family

Ingedients

  • 500g fresh gnocchi
  • 250g bag baby spinach
  • 100ml/3½ fl oz crème fraîche
  • 4 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 100g soft blue cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the gnocchi in a large pan of boiling water according to pack instructions.
    Plunge the spinach into the same pan, then immediately drain through a colander
    and shake off any excess water.
  2. Put the crème fraîche in a small ovenproof dish with the grated parmesan. Add the
    hot, drained gnocchi and spinach and give everything a good stir. Crumble the blue
    cheese over and season with ground pepper.
  3. lide the dish under a hot grill until the cheese is bubbling and golden. Serve immediately.

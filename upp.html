Pumpkin pasta

Mozzarella and pumpkin liven up pasta in this delicious Italian recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 125g ball mozzarella/li>
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • small handful sage leaves,
  • grated zest and juice of 1 unwaxed lemon
  • 500g/1lb 2oz piece pumpkin
  • 300g conchiglie pasta (shells)
  • 85g parmesan , finely grated

Steps

  1. Soften the garlic in 1 tbsp of the olive oil and leave to cool slightly. Put the
    mozzarella, butter, garlic, sage, lemon zest and juice into a food processor.
    Season and blend to a coarse paste, scraping down the sides with a spatula a
    few times.
  2. Transfer the paste to a sheet of parchment paper or cling film and roll into
    a cylinder. Chill for 30 mins or until firm enough to slice.
  3. Heat oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6. Pour remaining oil into a roasting tin and
    heat in the oven for 5 mins. Meanwhile, using a small sharp knife, peel the
    pumpkin, remove the seeds and cut flesh into 2cm cubes.
  4. Toss it in the hot oil, and season if you wish. Roast for 25-30 mins, turning
    occasionally.
  5. Cook the pasta according to pack instructions. Drain, return to the pan and tip
    in the pumpkin and parmesan. Slice or chop the mozzarella butter and toss into
    the hot pasta. Divide between 4 bowls, top with sage and serve.
